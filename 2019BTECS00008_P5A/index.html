Department Information

The Department of Chemistry - Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli was established in 1975. The department has contributed in producing several eminent engineers who are in key positions of the Government organizations, public sector and Multinational companies and have brought in significant contributions in various regions of India. The Department has a dedicated team of staff members and distinguished faculty, trained at renowned Institutes in India and abroad. The faculty members attract substantial financial support for their research activities from both governmental and private agencies.Alumni of the department can be found in all public and private sector organizations operating in diverse fields,holding senior key positions in the organizations.
